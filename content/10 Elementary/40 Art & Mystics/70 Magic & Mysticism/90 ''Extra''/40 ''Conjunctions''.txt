''Conjunctions''

like                              = как-будто, словно, как

''Birds froze in mid-air ~ and fell '''like''' stones to the ground.'' = %% Virginia Woolf, ''Orlando'' ~ LEGAMUS CORI

before                            = прежде чем, ~ до того как, ~ пока не

''Look '''before''' you leap.''

after                             = после того как

for                               = так как, поточу что, ~ ибо, поскольку

{For they are jolly good fellows} = <h3>'''''For''' they are jolly good fellows, ~ '''For''' they are jolly good fellows, ~ '''For''' they are jolly good fellows, ~ Which nobody can deny.''</h3> ~ LIBRIVOX
