idioms

: a can of worms -- a situation that is going to cause a lot of problems
: stone the crows -- an exclamation of surprise
: the top dog -- the person with the most power
: be the bee's knees -- to be very very good
: donkey’s years -- a long time
: have a cow -- to become very angry or upset about something
: have a frog in your throat -- you need to cough and force the air out in order to speak properly
: kill the fatted calf -- to celebrate the arrival of someone you haven’t seen for a long time
: like a bear with a sore head -- to be in a bad mood which causes you to complain a lot and to treat other people badly
