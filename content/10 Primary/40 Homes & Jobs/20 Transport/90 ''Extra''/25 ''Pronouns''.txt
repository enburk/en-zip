indefinite pronouns = неопределённые местоимения

===

any @ pronoun       = кто-нибудь, что-нибудь
some @ pronoun      = кое-кто, кое-что, ~ некоторые, одни, другие
each @ pronoun      = каждый, любой

===


===

something @@        = что-то, кое-что, что-нибудь
anything            = что-нибудь, что угодно
everything          = всё
nothing             = ничего, ничто

===

: This is nothing.
: Everything OK?
: Everything is known.
: Anything’s possible.
: Something must be done.

: There’s nothing to do.
: Nothing to do with me.
: I want to do ~ something great!
: Can any of you do ~ anything special?

===

: Two hours. One hundred and twenty minutes. Anything might be done in that time. Anything. Nothing.
: If you want something done right, ~ you have to do it yourself.

===

someone/somebody    = кто-то, кое-кто, кто-нибудь
anyone/anybody      = кто-нибудь, кто угодно
everyone/everybody  = все
no one/nobody       = никто

===

: Is anyone there?
: Is anybody home?
: Is there anybody here?
: You can't please everyone.

: Everybody knows that.
