modal verbs
===
'''will''' and '''would'''
===

: How '''would''' you like that?
: '''Would''' you like fries with that?
: Would you believe it?
: Ooh, I would.

: '''Will''' you do me a favour?
: Hey, '''will''' you do me a favour?
: '''Will''' you please do me a favour?
: '''Would''' you do me a favour please?

===
negative forms
would not → wouldn’t
could not → couldn’t
might not → mightn’t ~ {esp.} {Br.}
===

: I wouldn’t advise it.
