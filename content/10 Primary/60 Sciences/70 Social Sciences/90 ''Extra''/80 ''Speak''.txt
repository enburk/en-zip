speak, say, tell, talk

===
= '''speak''' используется в выражении ~ ''говорить на каком-либо языке''
===

: Do you speak English? @@
: Please speak to me in English ~ I do not understand French.

===
= '''speak''' подходит ~ для ситуаций монологов
===

: Let me speak ~ from the bottom ~ of my heart!

===
= '''speak''' подходит ~ для официальных ситуаций, ~ a '''talk''' – для нейтральных
===

: May I speak ~ to your accounts payable departament, ~ please?
: How can you tell?
: I'm gonna tell on you.

===
tell somebody something
===

: I tell you this...
: All right, my friend. ~ Now tell me. ~ What's the problem?

===
say something to somebody
===

: I should say...
: Say cheese!
: You can say that again!
