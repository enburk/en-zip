ways

===
someway/somehow           = каким-то образом
===

===
anyway/anyhow
{Am} {informal} ~ anyways = вообще
===

: What's that all about anyway?
: What's that all about anyways?

===
all the way               = все время, всю дорогу, ~ до конца
===

: His elevator doesn't go ~ all the way to the top.
: Her elevator does not go ~ all the way to the top.

===
the other way round
the other way around      = наоборот
===

: The thing is very exactly ~ the other way around.
: Brazil got its name from the nut, ~ not the other way around.
