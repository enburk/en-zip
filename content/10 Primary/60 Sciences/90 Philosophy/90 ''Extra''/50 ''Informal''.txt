informal ~ contractions

===
'til = until
===

: Can this wait 'til later?
: Can this please wait 'til later?
: Wait 'til your father gets home!

===
'twas = it was
===

: Yes, 'twas so.

===
'em = them
===

: Book'em!
: Go get 'em!
: Cuff'em!
: We’re really goin’ to show ’em.

===
'cause = because
===

: 'Cause I want to!

===
ain't = am not, ~ are not, is not, ~ have not, has not
===

: If it ain't broke don't fix it.
