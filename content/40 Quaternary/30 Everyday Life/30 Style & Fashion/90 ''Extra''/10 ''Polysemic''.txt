polysemic

***
cling @ adhere = to adhere to an object
: The dress really suits you and your coloring. The way it clings.
cling @ hold   = to hold very tightly, ~ as to not fall off
cling @ sound  = to produce a high-pitched ringing sound, like a small bell

***
lean @ slim    = slim, not fleshy
lean @ bend    = to incline, deviate, or bend, ~ from a vertical position
: Time to lean, time to clean.
: If you have time to lean, you have time to clean.
