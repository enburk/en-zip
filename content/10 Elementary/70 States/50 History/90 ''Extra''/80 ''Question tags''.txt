question tags\\disjunctive questions = расчлененные вопросы

===
= переводятся: ~ не правда ли? ~ не так ли? да?
===

: Funny weather we’re having, ~ isn’t it?
: You don't really say that, ~ do you?

===
= обратите внимание на ответ:
===

: “This blouse is ugly, isn’t it?” ~ “Yes, [it is].”
: “This blouse is ugly, isn’t it?” ~ “No, [it isn’t], it’s nice."

: “You don’t smoke, do you?" ~ “No, [I don’t]."
: “You don’t smoke, do you?" ~ “Yes, [I do]."
