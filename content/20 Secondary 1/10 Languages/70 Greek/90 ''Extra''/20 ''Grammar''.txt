phrasal verbs     # HEAD

---
turn loose        = to release or let go
: When I turn you both loose, go
---
turn out @ result = to result, end up
: And so it turned out to be.
: How did it turn out?
: Everything will turn out ~ right in the end.
---
turn out @ off    = switch off
: Please, turn off your cell phones.
: Say, what happened to the lights
