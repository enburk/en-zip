discontinuity


noun   (plural discontinuities)


1. A lack of continuity, regularity or sequence; a break or gap. {{defdate|from 16th c.}}


2. (mathematics) A point in the range of a function at which it is undefined or discontinuous. {{defdate|from 19th c.}}


3. (geology) a subterranean interface at which seismic velocities change

Derived terms:
Gutenberg discontinuity
Mohorovičić discontinuity
