Australianisms

Emu in the Sky = an Aboriginal constellation

: We also learned how to pronounce things with an Australian accent. For example

---
bludger
= a pimp, a man living off the earnings of a harlot
= a person who avoids working, or doing their share of work
: I bludge, Thou bludgest, He bludges, We bludge, You bludge, They all bludge.
