basic ~ vocabulary

---
ache = боль
: I've got a stomach ache.
: I've got a tummy ache.
: Not tonight dear, ~ I have a headache.
: I'm very brave generally, only to-day I happen to have a headache.

---
feel = чувствовать себя
: I don't feel well.
: I don't feel very good.
: I don't feel so good.
: Well, how do you think I feel?

===
sick = больные, болезнь, ~ болезненный, нездоровый, ~ чувствующий тошноту
I feel sick.
I feel kind of sick.
: He's sick? How does he think I feel?
===
: Sick!
